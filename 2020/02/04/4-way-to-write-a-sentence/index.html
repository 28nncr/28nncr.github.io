Class practice

Compound writing for 4 different types

  1. I must study. If not I will fail.

    1. I must study**, or** I will fail.
    2. I must study**; otherwise,** I will fail.
    3. If I don’t study, I will fail.
    4. Without studying, I will fail.

  2. I’m staying home. The weather is bad.

    1. The weather is bad**, so** I’m staying home.
    2. The weather is bad**; therefore,** I’m staying home.
    3. Because the weather is bad, I’m staying home.
    4. Due to/Because of the bad weather, I’m staying home.

  3. I went to school. I didn’t study.

    1. I went to school**, but** I didn’t study.
    2. I went to school**; however,** I didn’t study.
    3. Even though I went to school, I didn’t study.
    4. Despite/In spite of going to school, I didn’t study.
