Sex Education: Should Be Deeper into People's Consciousness

Posted on 2019-11-09 Edited on 2019-12-30 In TV Series

This article went to recommended a TV Series called Sex Education. It released in 2019 on Netflix.

Attention: Spoiler Included

The first time, I saw the title of the TV series. And I guessed it might include lots of naked sense, but it was wrong. The director skillfully eliminated sexual images in audiences’ minds. It more prefers to education, not sex sense in the TV series. We can learn about characters grow up and how to solve the sexual problem when in puberty.

I hope that Otis and Ola can become a couple at the end. Ola is an outgoing and upright girl. She can understand what Otis needs. Otis was afraid of showing in public and fear of sexual. Ola can tolerate his mistakes and begin a new life in the Episode #1.8.

Eric, who is the coolest, bravest and kindest person in Otis’s mind and his best friend dressed the drag queen makeup in the bus station. And the guys who were homophobes physically assault for Eric. Then Eric got hurt. But one day, he saw a guy, who dressed drag queen’s clothes the same with him, asked Eric’s road very confident, so he recovered his self-confident. In the dancing party night, episode #1.7, Eric, who dressed the drag queen makeup, talked to his father in front of the school gate. His dad was so worried about his makeup. Eric response, “Fear cannot help me. It makes me weak.”, his dad“Why do you have to be so much?”, Eric said, “This is me. I will be hurt either way. Isn’t it better to be who I am?”. The answer was so simple and clear. Just do ourselves. Like Eric, don’t worry about what other people think.

Episode #1.5 was my favorite episode in Sex Education. Someone reveals Ruby’s vagina in public. And the principal talked about this in the auditorium. And the bad guy shows her name to the public. And her friends went to defense her. And to said,”it is my vagina!”. Even the boy was coming to help Ruby. This scene was so powerful for me.

It does not show too much sex in the TV series, more education for us. But in some countries’ high school should more work hard on it. Do the best sex education for the teenager. The teenager will know what should they do, what should not do during puberty.
